Dungeoneer Design Document

Game Description

Dungeoneer is a top down, 2D, rogue-like adventure game in which you play a character who's goal is to escape from a dungeon by any means necessary. With procedurally generated floors and rooms, the player will never quite know what to expect each time they play. Randomly spawned enemies will try to stop the player from getting to the end of each floor and escaping the dungeon. But don't worry, the player finds plenty of weapons and other loot with which to defeat the monsters, just not always what they need.
